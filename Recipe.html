My steak recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp (15 mL) mixed peppercorns or black peppercorns. Did you buy this ingredient?
  • 2 beef grilling medallions, about 1 inch thick (6 oz/175 g each). Did you buy this ingredient?
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) vegetable oil. Did you buy this ingredient?
  • 1/4 tsp (1 mL) salt. Did you buy this ingredient?

Directions

  • Using mortar and pestle or in resealable plastic bag using bottom of pot, crush peppercorns. Have you completed this step?
  • Brush steaks all over with oil; coat with peppercorns. Have you completed this step?
  • In large skillet, fry steaks over high heat, turning once, until desired doneness, 10 minutes for medium-rare. Have you completed this step?
  • Sprinkle with salt. Transfer to warm plate; tent with foil and let stand for 5 minutes before slicing across the grain. Have you completed this step?
